Welcome Message

Welcome to Soul Fire!

Soul Fire is a band on YouTube. We aim to worship God through song. We are a small band, currently consisting of only 2 members. We are a non-profit group, but if you would like to support our work you can do so here.

LIKE SHARE SUBSCRIBE
By liking our videos you are telling the YouTube algorithm to recommend this to others. By sharing our videos you are spreading the word about the salvation of the Lord. Your share could directly impact someone's life! By subscribing you will be reminded when we release new videos. You can even choose to get notifications for new videos!
